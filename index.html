ACM-W India seeks to take forward the task of the ACM community, but with a particular focus on the empowerment of women in computing in India.

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the contributions of technical women.

ABOUT ACM-W

Greetings from ACM STUDENTS CHAPTER...!

We are excited to announce a new association “ACM-W” for the academic year 2021-2022.

ACM welcomes any women who is passionate enough to pursue excellence in any field of Computer Science, irrespective of their year, branch or CGPA.

ACM W encourages people to gain and impart knowledge about Computing and contribute in the fields of research and development.

Here at ACM Women the mission is to create a thriving Computing culture at Vellore-AP by sharing knowledge and ideas and promoting and nurturing the interest and curiosity of students in relevant fields.

The ACM-W aims to be active by organizing events to engage the students in a happy and glorious way with fun-filled moments.

F.A.Q

  • Why ACM-W is Started ?

    ACM Celebrations bring women from your local region together for networking and build a feeling of community.
    ACM-W provides financial and conference services to support to these conferences.

  • When was ACM-W Started ?

    ACM Celebrations began in 2004 in India.
    Today there are more than 30 Celebrations worldwide with more than 5000 participants per year.

  • What are the outcomes of ACM chapter?

    Each celebration is unique. But common activities include poster sessions, technical presentations, industry and graduate school panels, and career fairs.
